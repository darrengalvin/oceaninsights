### 1. **Bubble Rise** ‚≠ê TOP PICK
**Concept**: Guide rising bubbles from the ocean floor to the surface

**Gameplay:**
- Bubbles rise automatically from bottom
- **Breathe IN** (4 sec) ‚Üí Bubbles rise faster, glow brighter
- **Breathe OUT** (6 sec) ‚Üí Bubbles slow down, become translucent
- Tilt phone to guide bubbles around obstacles (kelp, rocks, submarines)
- Collect bonus items (starfish, pearls) by timing breaths
- Reach surface without popping bubbles

**Why It Works:**
- Breathing IS the core mechanic (not just a pause)
- Natural ocean/submarine theme
- Meditative but engaging
- Visual feedback on breath control
- Can be competitive (time trials) or zen (endless mode)

**Mental Health Integration:**
- **Breathing Practice**: Players learn diaphragmatic breathing
- **Visual Biofeedback**: See breath control in real-time
- **Affirmations**: Messages on collected items
- **Calm + Challenge**: Perfect balance
- **Progress Tracking**: "You've taken 500 mindful breaths this week"

**Technical Implementation:**
```dart
// Use phone microphone or manual button
- Hold screen = Breathing in
- Release = Breathing out
// OR
- Tap "Breathe" rhythm indicator
- Visual breathing circle (already in app!)
```
