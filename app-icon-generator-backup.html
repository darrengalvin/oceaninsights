Ocean Insight App Icon

How to Use:

  1. Right-click the icon below
  2. Select "Save Image As..." or "Download Image"
  3. Save as ocean-insight-icon.png
  4. Upload to appicon.co
  5. Download the generated assets
  6. Replace contents of ios/Runner/Assets.xcassets/AppIcon.appiconset/
Icon will be saved at 1024×1024 pixels (required for App Store)
