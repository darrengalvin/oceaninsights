3. The Pay It Forward Model

Below the Surface operates on a unique community-supported model:

  • The App is free to download and use - There is no upfront purchase required
  • Your access has been covered - Someone before you in the chain contributed to make this possible
  • You are invited to continue the chain - You can choose to contribute to cover someone else's access
  • No one is left behind - Those who genuinely cannot afford to pay (teenagers, those between jobs, families in difficulty) still receive full access and support

This is an honesty-based system. The App continues to exist because enough people choose to contribute when they can.
