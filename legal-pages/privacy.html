Your privacy is our priority. We collect minimal data and it never leaves your device.

Important Medical Disclaimer

Ocean Insight is designed for general mental wellbeing support only. This app is NOT a replacement for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis or emergency, please seek immediate professional help.

Data Collection

We collect minimal information to provide you with a personalised experience:

  • User Type: Your selected category (e.g., Submariner, Veteran) - stored locally only
  • Age Bracket: Your selected age range - stored locally only
  • App Usage Data: Mood logs, goals, ritual completions - all stored locally on your device

This information never leaves your device and cannot be used to identify you.

What We Don't Do

  • No personal identity collection (name, email, phone number)
  • No GPS or location tracking
  • No camera or microphone access
  • No contact list access
  • No advertisements
  • No subscription fees
  • No data sharing with third parties
  • No tracking for marketing purposes

Content Synchronisation

The app connects to our servers to download educational content, scenarios, and protocols. This connection:

  • Downloads content updates when you have internet access
  • Does not upload any personal data
  • Is not required for core app functionality (works offline)

Local Data Storage

All personal data (mood logs, goals, settings, preferences) is stored locally on your device using secure local storage. This data:

  • Stays on your device only
  • Is never transmitted to external servers
  • Is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Can be manually deleted at any time through the app's "Manage Your Data" section

Your Right to Delete Data

You can delete all your data at any time:

  • Go to the app's home screen
  • Tap "Your Data" at the bottom
  • Select "Delete All My Data"
  • Confirm by typing DELETE

This permanently removes all locally stored data. You can also simply delete the app to remove all data.

In-App Purchases

Ocean Insight offers optional "Pay It Forward" contributions. These purchases:

  • Are processed securely through Apple App Store or Google Play
  • Are entirely optional - you are never required to pay
  • Help cover access for others who genuinely can't afford it
  • Support the ongoing development and maintenance of the app
  • We do not store your payment information

Children's Privacy

Ocean Insight is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or the app, please contact us:

  • Email: support@oceaninsight.app
  • Through the App Store or Google Play Store listing
