Frequently Asked Questions

Does this app work offline?
Yes! All core features work completely offline. This includes breathing exercises, mood tracking, daily rituals, goals, affirmations, and games. Educational content syncs automatically when you have an internet connection, but previously downloaded content remains available offline.
Is my data private?
Absolutely. All your personal data (mood logs, goals, settings) stays on your device only. We don't collect personal information, and nothing is uploaded to external servers. If you delete the app, all your data is permanently removed. You can also manually delete all data through the app's "Manage Your Data" section.
Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store according to their respective policies. To request a refund, please contact the store where you made your purchase. Apple typically allows refunds within 14 days, and Google within 48 hours for immediate refunds or longer through their support process.
How does Pay It Forward work?
Someone before you contributed to cover your access to Ocean Insight. When you choose to Pay It Forward, you're covering access for someone else who genuinely can't afford it - like a teenager who needs mental health support but has no income, or someone facing financial hardship. It's an honesty-based system that keeps the chain going so everyone who needs help can receive it. Contributions are processed securely through the App Store or Google Play.
How do I delete all my data?
You can delete all your data at any time:
  1. Go to the app's home screen
  2. Scroll down and tap "Your Data"
  3. Tap "Delete All My Data"
  4. Type DELETE to confirm
Alternatively, simply uninstalling the app will remove all data from your device.
Is this app a replacement for therapy?
No. Ocean Insight is designed to support your mental wellbeing with helpful tools and resources, but it is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you're struggling with mental health issues, please consult a qualified healthcare provider. If you're in crisis, please contact emergency services immediately.
What devices does the app support?
Ocean Insight is available on:
  • iOS: iPhone and iPad running iOS 14.0 or later
  • Android: Devices running Android 6.0 (API level 23) or later
Can I transfer my data to a new phone?
Since data is stored locally on your device, transferring to a new phone depends on your backup method:
  • iPhone to iPhone: Use iCloud backup or direct transfer during setup
  • Android to Android: Use Google backup or manufacturer's transfer tool
  • Cross-platform: Data cannot be transferred between iOS and Android
