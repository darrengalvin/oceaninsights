Features

Everything You Need, Nothing You Don't

Built with service members in mind. Simple, effective tools that respect your privacy and work anywhere.

Breathing Exercises

Guided techniques including Navy SEAL box breathing. Reduce stress, improve focus, and find calm in any situation.

Mood Tracking

Log how you feel throughout the day. Recognise patterns and triggers to better understand yourself.

Daily Rituals

Build positive habits with customisable morning and evening routines. Choose topics that matter to you.

Learn & Grow

Educational content about brain function, psychology, and evidence-based wellbeing strategies.

Mindful Games

Relaxing games designed to reduce stress. Zen garden, bubble rise, block stacking, and more.

Works Offline

All core features work without internet connection. Perfect for submarines, ships, or remote locations.

Our Model

Pay It Forward

Your access has been covered by someone before you. Now you can keep the chain going for someone who genuinely needs it.

1

Someone Covered You

A previous user contributed to ensure you could access these tools freely.

2

Use It Freely

All features are available to you. No subscriptions, no locked content, no strings attached.

3

Cover Someone Else

If you can, contribute to cover access for teenagers, those in hardship, or anyone who needs support.

This is an honesty system. There is no obligation to contribute. The app exists because enough people choose to keep the chain going.

Important Disclaimer

Below the Surface is designed for general mental wellbeing support only. This app is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact emergency services or a mental health professional immediately.
